About Me

Hi! Thanks for stopping by. I'm Karen, and I enjoy collaboration and solving challenging problems. Currently, I'm working as a Software Consultant who is passionate about user experience and hoping to further my career in application and website design.

As a recent graduate of the University of Virginia with a double major in Computer Science and Cognitive Science, I finally found a field that marries the two together: technical design. Cognitive Science fueled my interests in how people think and behave, while Computer Science piques my interest in the latest tech. My goal is to improve people's lives through positive design experiences.

To me, user experience design is all about creating meaningful experiences to the user - without difficulty and second thought. I'm constantly learning and adapting in my work, and I value keeping an open mind.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy traveling, trying different types of food and blogging about my food experiences. You can also catch me on the mat, doing yoga or pilates!
